About Us

The Spatial Cognition and Autonomous Learning Laboratory (ScaleLab) focuses on the core direction of embodied intelligence, emphasizing on the study of the embodied brain for spatial cognition and long-sequence reasoning, as well as the embodied cerebellum with high-frequency response, high precision and high dexterity. Under the theoretical framework of autonomous learning and continuous learning, we are committed to developing the next generation of embodied intelligent robots. The goal of our Group is to build a general embodied AI system that can learn optimal policy efficiently and can be generalized well in unseen tasks and environments. My research slogan: Shape the intelligence, Spirit the machine! (为智慧塑形，为机器注灵！) For more information, please visit our research page, or follow us on Github.
We are always looking for self-motivated PhD students, master students and research assistants. To join our lab, please see the Join Us page.


DeepTimber Community

We are the founder of DeepTimber, a large community of engineers and researchers who share the interests on embodied AI and robotics. Discuss the latest research, share your projects, and connect with others who are just as enthusiastic as you are. Our community welcomes everyone, from beginners to experts, so don't hesitate to join us and start exploring the exciting world of embodied AI and robotics today.
